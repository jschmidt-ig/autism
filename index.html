Unified Care. Smarter Tools. Better Lives.

Innovative Solutions Initiative (ISI) is transforming care delivery with Oria — AI-powered support for families and providers.

The Problem

Healthcare is fragmented. Data lives in silos, transitions break down, and manual processes overwhelm families and professionals. The result: lost time, lost outcomes, and a system too complex for those it’s meant to serve.

Our Solution: Oria

What Oria Does

  • Summarizes medical and therapy documents
  • Detects anomalies in health and behavior
  • Suggests personalized care plans
  • Automates intake assessments

Value for Providers

  • Faster onboarding
  • Fewer documentation errors
  • Real-time insights for better decisions

MyEMR Platform

A secure, patient-facing system that gives individuals ownership of their care journey. HIPAA/FERPA/GDPR compliant, consent-driven, and integrated with EHRs and school systems.

Impact & Growth

  • Accelerates clinical trials by matching patients faster
  • Reduces healthcare costs through automation
  • Improves public health readiness by spotting trends early
