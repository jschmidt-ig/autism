Unified Care. Smarter Tools. Better Lives.

Innovative Solutions Initiative (ISI) builds Oria — an AI-powered platform to integrate healthcare, education, and social services. We deliver clear insights, smarter workflows, and care that works for people.

The Problem

Care today is fragmented. Data is siloed across systems. Families and providers struggle to coordinate. Manual work abounds.

  • Professionals waste hours managing disconnected records
  • Individuals lack control and visibility to their care data
  • Transitions between services cause error and inefficiency
  • Care systems are difficult to navigate for patients and caregivers

The system breaks down under complexity, leaving people behind.

Our Solution: Oria

Oria is an AI engine trained to read, interpret, and explain medical & educational documents. It ingests sources like IEPs, therapy notes, clinical records, assessments, and more — then outputs summaries and tailored care paths.

What Oria Delivers

  • Document summaries and clear care recommendations
  • Anomaly detection to flag hidden issues
  • Personalized care plans based on real data
  • Automated intake assessments to reduce overhead

Why Providers Use It

  • Quicker onboarding of clients
  • Fewer documentation errors and redundancies
  • Decision support when it matters

Platform: MyEMR & Interoperability

MyEMR gives individuals a secure, connected dashboard. Permissions, sharing, and workflows are managed with privacy built-in. Integrated with EHRs and educational systems, MyEMR bridges gaps, not create new ones.

Core Features

  • Role-based access for clinicians, educators, caregivers
  • Consent-driven sharing and emergency protocols
  • Seamless integration with healthcare and school systems

Interoperability Tools

  • FHIR APIs for modern data exchange
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • Bulk data export for scale
  • CDS Hooks to embed decision support

Impact & Growth
