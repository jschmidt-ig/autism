MyEMR Platform

MyEMR is our patient-facing system designed to give individuals ownership of their care journey. It is fully HIPAA, FERPA, and GDPR compliant and ensures that data is secure, accessible, and shareable with the right people at the right time.

Core Features

  • Role-based permissions for providers, families, and educators
  • Consent-based data sharing and emergency access protocols
  • Integration with EHRs and school systems for seamless workflows

Interoperability Protocols

  • FHIR API (Epic) for secure, real-time data exchange
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure patient authentication
  • Bulk Data Support for scalable access to large records
  • CDS Hooks to integrate clinical decision support tools
